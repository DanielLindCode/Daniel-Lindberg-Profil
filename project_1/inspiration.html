Inspiration

One of my biggest passions and hobbies are gaming and "geek culture". When you game every day or watch a lot of moves you can’t help but get inspired from these works. I really enjoy lore heavy universes where you can deep dive into the history and characters of that world. It inspires me when I do my own world building. Something more relatable to programing is all the success stories of single individuals or small groups of people that work hard on their projects and make it big in the end. For example, is the story of Markus Persson (Minecraft creator) really inspiring to me!
